Find out if you’re eligible for New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance

To be eligible you’ll need to:

  • have worked as an employee
  • paid Class 1 National Insurance contributions, usually in the last 2 to 3 years

National Insurance credits can also count.

You will not be eligible if you were:

  • self-employed
  • only paid Class 2 National Insurance contributions

Unless you were working as a share fisherman or a volunteer development worker.

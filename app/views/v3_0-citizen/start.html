Apply for New Style Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)

Before you start

To be eligible for New Style Jobseeker's Allowance (JSA) you must:

  • live in England, Scotland or Wales
  • be looking for work
  • have been employed and paid enough National Insurance contributions over the last 2 to 3 years (National Insurance credits also count)
  • be under State Pension age

You are not eligible for New Style JSA if you get severe disability premium or got it in the last month and are still eligible.

Before you apply

You will need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • details of the bank or building society account you want your benefit paid in to
  • details of your recent employment, including dates you were employed and employer contact details
  • details of pensions you receive
